[96/173] NamedBreakpoints(命名断点) [汉化反馈]

If there are too many breakpoints in your application, ease the search by giving them names.

To add a name to a breakpoint, show the list of all breakpoints (&shortcut:ViewBreakpoints;), right-click the one you are interested in, and edit its description.
